I wanted to do a remake of a level, so I decided to take my favorite Quak2 CTF map and convert it to TFC. It isn't complete yet at all, but I wanted to put up some pictures, just because I think they are cool.
SS_Nyx_L - Download






SS_Nyx is my second map for TFC. It was the first completed map I have ever made. I am very proud of it and it will be played in STA during Dec '02. It's a vanilla CTF map, but if the defense holds the flag for 5 minutes, (quite a hard feat to do on this map), then they score 5 points for the team. It took me a few months to build, because I was learning how to use Worldcraft at the same time as making the map. I also took large breaks to think about layout and other things. Anyway, I think it's a good map, other people think the same, and it has gotten good reviews from sites, so I think you would like it also.
